about me

I am a lot of things.

After university in 2017, I worked as a Furniture Designer catering to high-end residential and commercial clients that honed my 3D modeling and rendering skills. When the pandemic came in during 2020, I started freelancing on the side as a 3D animator that taps small businesses requiring fun and captivating social media content which eventually led me to create static design content and social media branding for my clients. After four years in the furniture industry and a year of mixing it with my freelance design gig, I eventually landed a position as an Associate Art Director for a Canadian-based startup advertising agency.
